Purist Remarks

Diamond edition utilised rotoscoping that allegedly impacted Tinkerbell's glow and fairy dust, but colors might be most accurate. The 2002 DVD is unrestored with colder colors. The 1991 LD has different coloring, and allegedly more details in the artwork.
