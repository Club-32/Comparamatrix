Winnie the Pooh and His Story Too (HD, 9 minutes):

A short history of Winnie the Pooh. 		 

Deleted Scenes Introduced by the Directors (HD, 15 minutes)

Five deleted scenes: "The Tummy Song", "Rabbit's Friends and Relations", "Original Eeyore Intro", "Original Tigger Intro" and "Pooh Searches for a Tail". 		Only three of the five deleted scenes are included.

Bonus Shorts (HD, 8 minutes)

"The Ballad of Nessie" — released theatrically alongside the movie — and "Mini Adventures of Winnie the Pooh: Pooh's Balloon".

Disney Song Selection (HD, 11 minutes)

Sing along to "Winnie the Pooh Theme Song", "The Tummy Song", "A Very Important Thing to Do", "The Backson Song", "It's Gonna Be Great", "Everything is Honey" and "The Winner Song Finale". 		 

Sing Along With the Movie (HD)

Sing along during the movie.

How to design a Winnie the Pooh nursery.
