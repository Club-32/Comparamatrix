Purist Remarks

Aggressively restored twice (2003, 2008). Second restoration has vibrant colors and the original 2.55:1 aspect ratio. The 1987 CAV LD also has these accurate colors (?) but is pan-and-scan. The 1997 CAV LD is widescreen, but brither and duller colors (same goes for the 2003 DVD).
