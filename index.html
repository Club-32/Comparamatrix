The Disney Comparamatrix

Comparison of home video releases of Disney animated classics

There is a disturbing trend of moving bonus features to a digital only platform. I am not taking these in account.

Insights from the "Recommended Editions of Disney Animated Features" thread on Original Trilogy are taken into account.

You can notify me of corrections or suggest changes through this project's Github repository.

Key

Color Meaning
  The best version of a feature.
  A somewhat lesser version of a feature.
  A seriously condensed version of a feature.
  Things I don't really consider of actual importance.
  Strong uncertainties. For LaserDisc these are sometimes simply left uncolored.
